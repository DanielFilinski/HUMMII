# Privacy Policy - Hummii

**Last Updated:** January 2025  
**Effective Date:** January 2025

---

## Introduction

Hummii ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service marketplace platform located at https://hummii.ca (the "Service").

This policy complies with the **Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA)** and other applicable Canadian privacy laws.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide to Us

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, profile picture
- **Contractor Information:** Business details, verification documents, certifications, hourly rates
- **Order Information:** Service requests, messages, reviews, ratings
- **Payment Information:** Payment method details (processed securely through Stripe)
- **Identity Verification:** Government-issued ID for contractor verification (processed through Stripe Identity)

### 1.2 Information Automatically Collected

- **Device Information:** IP address, device type, operating system, browser type
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, time spent, features used, search queries
- **Location Data:** General location based on IP address (with your consent)
- **Cookies and Tracking Technologies:** See our [Cookie Policy](cookie-policy-en.md)

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties

- **OAuth Providers:** Information from Google Sign-In (email, name, profile picture)
- **Payment Processors:** Transaction data from Stripe
- **Identity Verification:** Verification status from Stripe Identity

## 4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

- **Encryption:** TLS 1.3 encryption for data in transit
- **Database Security:** Encrypted at rest with SSL connections
- **Access Controls:** Limited access on a need-to-know basis
- **Regular Audits:** Security audits and vulnerability assessments
- **Password Security:** Bcrypt hashing (cost factor 12+)
- **Token Security:** HTTP-only cookies, short-lived access tokens

## 5. Your Rights (PIPEDA Compliance)

Under Canadian privacy law, you have the following rights:

### 5.1 Right to Access
- Request a copy of all personal information we hold about you
- **How to Exercise:** Use the "Export My Data" feature in your account settings or contact us

### 5.2 Right to Rectification
- Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
- **How to Exercise:** Update your profile in account settings or contact us

### 5.3 Right to Erasure
- Request deletion of your account and personal information
- **How to Exercise:** Use "Delete Account" in account settings or contact us
- **Note:** Some information may be retained for legal compliance (e.g., payment records for 7 years)

### 5.4 Right to Data Portability
- Receive your data in a machine-readable format (JSON)
- **How to Exercise:** Use "Export My Data" feature in account settings

### 5.5 Right to Object
- Opt-out of marketing communications
- **How to Exercise:** Update preferences in account settings or unsubscribe from emails

### 5.6 Right to Withdraw Consent
- Withdraw consent for processing (may limit service functionality)
- **How to Exercise:** Contact us or update preferences in account settings

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for the following periods:

- **Account Data:** Until account deletion or 3 years of inactivity
- **Payment Records:** 7 years (Canadian tax law requirement - CRA)
- **Chat Messages:** 90 days, then automatic deletion
- **Audit Logs:** 1 year minimum (PIPEDA compliance)
- **Identity Verification:** Until account deletion or verification expiration (2 years)
