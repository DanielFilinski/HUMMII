## Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Hummii ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website and platform (https://hummii.ca). This policy should be read in conjunction with our [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy-en.md).

---

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device and store some information about your preferences or past actions.

---

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

### 2.1 Essential Cookies (Required)

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off.

**Purpose:**
- Authentication and session management
- Security (CSRF protection)
- Load balancing

**Examples:**
- `accessToken` - JWT access token (HTTP-only, 15 minutes)
- `refreshToken` - JWT refresh token (HTTP-only, 7 days)
- Session cookies for user authentication

**Retention:** Session-based or as specified

---

### 2.2 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization.

**Purpose:**
- Remember your preferences (language, region)
- Remember your login state
- Provide personalized features

**Examples:**
- `locale` - Language preference (English/French)
- `theme` - UI theme preference
- `cookiePreferences` - Your cookie consent choices

**Retention:** Up to 1 year

---

### 2.3 Analytics Cookies (Optional)

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website.

**Purpose:**
- Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
- Improve website performance
- Understand user behavior

**Examples:**
- Google Analytics cookies
- Platform analytics cookies

**Retention:** Up to 2 years

**Note:** You can opt-out of analytics cookies in your cookie preferences.

---

### 2.4 Marketing Cookies (Optional)

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and track campaign performance.

**Purpose:**
- Show relevant ads to users
- Measure ad campaign effectiveness
- Prevent showing the same ad repeatedly

**Examples:**
- Advertising platform cookies
- Social media tracking pixels

**Retention:** Up to 1 year

**Note:** You can opt-out of marketing cookies in your cookie preferences.

## 4. Cookie Management

### 4.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit Hummii, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can:

- Accept all cookies
- Reject optional cookies (analytics, marketing)
- Customize your cookie preferences

Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for the platform to function.

### 4.2 Managing Your Preferences

You can update your cookie preferences at any time by:

1. **Account Settings:** Go to Settings → Privacy → Cookie Preferences
2. **Cookie Banner:** Click "Manage Preferences" in the cookie banner
3. **Contact Us:** Email privacy@hummii.ca

### 4.3 Browser Settings

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
- **Firefox:** Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
- **Edge:** Settings → Privacy → Cookies

**Note:** Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

- **Control cookie usage:** Opt-out of optional cookies
- **Access your data:** Export your cookie preferences
- **Delete cookies:** Clear cookies from your browser
- **Withdraw consent:** Change cookie preferences at any time
